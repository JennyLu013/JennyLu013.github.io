Research Highlights

Chrono-Controlled Hydrogel (SeqGel) for TNBC Immuno-Metabolic Therapy

2024 – 2025

  • Designed injectable PEG hydrogel enabling size-gated sequential release: fast DCA priming → sustained PED@tCu nanosheet delivery
  • Demonstrated metabolic–immune synergy with increased PDH activity, lactate reduction, mitochondrial complex II inhibition, and downstream AMPK activation leading to PD-L1 degradation and MHC-I upregulation
  • In 4T1 models, achieved strong tumour growth inhibition and marked reduction of lung/lymph-node metastases
  • Generated systemic immune memory with increased tumour-infiltrating CD8⁺ T cells

Mitochondria-Targeted CuET (TPP-CuET) for Immunogenic Cuproptosis

2022 – 2024

  • Engineered TPP-modified CuET with optimal log P and enhanced mitochondrial copper accumulation
  • Established hallmarks of immunogenic cuproptosis (CRT exposure, ATP/HMGB1 release) and macrophage M1 polarisation
  • Transcriptomics showed MHC-I pathway upregulation
  • Validated activation of both innate and adaptive immunity, motivating combination immunotherapy strategies

CuET to Overcome Cisplatin Resistance

2021 – 2022

  • Demonstrated GSH-resistant redox profile; IC₅₀ in A549/DDP at nM level vs μM for CDDP
  • Built BSA-stabilised CuET nanoparticles: high loading, improved solubility, stable >8 weeks at 4°C
  • Showed potent in vivo antitumour efficacy with favourable safety profile

Mechanisms & Delivery Strategies of Disulfiram/Cu²⁺ (DSF/Cu²⁺) in Oncology

2020 – 2021

  • Mapped five core delivery strategies; analysed 21 clinical trials to identify translation bottlenecks (tumour accumulation, dosing)
  • Proposed nanomedicine solutions and highlighted immunotherapy-enabling modalities
